  • Medium Bird Toys

    Medium bird toys are great for Caiques, Conures, Lories, Pionus, Ringnecks and Quakers. Chewing is a major activity in the wild, especially during breeding season when birds are actively burrowing nests into tree trunks. It is imperative that birds be provided with destructible toys. For medium sized birds, toys designed with balsa, soft pine, bird-safe cardboard and vine based materials are excellent choices.
