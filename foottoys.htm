  • Foot Toys

    Foot toys are especially great for baby birds across all size categories. A parrot's foot is very adept at holding and grasping small items. Foot toys are small toys designed to be held easily so your parrot can enjoy manipulating and chewing various components. Providing foot toys promotes good foot dexterity as well as an excellent outlet for fun! Create a "toy chest" out of a cardboard box or empty dish to store hand toys in the bottom of your birds cage.
